SPROUTSENSE - 10 MIN PROFESSIONAL PRESENTATION GUIDE
====================================================

Total Duration: 10 minutes
Slides: 10
Goal: Clear, confident, and demo-focused delivery

SLIDE 1 (0:00-0:40) - "SproutSense: Intelligent Plant Care, End to End"
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Core message:
- We built a full IoT + AI system that monitors plant health, automates watering, and detects disease risk early.

Speaker notes:
- "SproutSense combines embedded systems, cloud backend, and AI assistance into one practical plant-care platform."

SLIDE 2 (0:40-1:20) - "The Real Problem: Plants Fail Quietly"
---------------------------------------------------------------
Core message:
- Most plant loss happens because stress signals are missed.
- Manual watering is inconsistent and disease signs are detected too late.

Speaker notes:
- "Our focus is not only automation, but timely decision support before visible damage appears."

SLIDE 3 (1:20-2:20) - "Architecture at a Glance: Dual ESP32 + MERN"
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Core message:
- ESP32-SENSOR board handles sensing, pumping, and local safety controls.
- ESP32-CAM handles image capture and disease classification.
- Node/Express + MongoDB + React dashboard complete the pipeline.

Speaker notes:
- "We split responsibilities across two boards for reliability and smoother real-time performance."

SLIDE 4 (2:20-3:20) - "Hardware Stack Built for Reliability"
--------------------------------------------------------------
Show:
- ESP32 Dev Board
- ESP32-CAM
- DHT22, soil moisture sensor, LDR, flow sensor
- Relay module + external pump power

Speaker notes:
- "Sensor fidelity and electrical safety were major design priorities, especially around pump control."

SLIDE 5 (3:20-4:20) - "Data Journey: From Leaf to Dashboard"
-------------------------------------------------------------
Core flow:
- Edge devices collect telemetry and events.
- Backend APIs process and persist into MongoDB.
- Frontend dashboard visualizes live state, trends, and controls.

Speaker notes:
- "This closed-loop flow enables both monitoring and immediate control actions from one interface."

SLIDE 6 (4:20-5:20) - "Autonomous Watering with Safety Guardrails"
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Core message:
- Watering is triggered by thresholds and system rules.
- Runtime and volume checks prevent overwatering.
- Every cycle is logged for traceability and analytics.

Speaker notes:
- "Automation is only useful when it is safe, measurable, and easy to audit."

SLIDE 7 (5:20-6:30) - "On-Device Reliability and Safety"
-----------------------------------------------------------
Show:
- Serial diagnostics output from ESP32-SENSOR
- Moisture threshold trigger behavior
- Pump safety guards (target volume and max runtime)

Speaker notes:
- "The device logic prioritizes reliability: threshold-based watering, runtime limits, and continuous telemetry updates to the backend."

SLIDE 8 (6:30-7:30) - "AI Vision Pipeline: Detect Before Damage"
------------------------------------------------------------------
Core message:
- ESP32-CAM captures leaf images.
- AI classification pipeline predicts disease categories and confidence.
- Alerts are pushed to backend and surfaced in the dashboard.

Speaker notes:
- "This shifts plant care from reactive treatment to proactive intervention."

SLIDE 9 (7:30-8:40) - "Live Demo: 60-Second System Walkthrough"
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Demo script:
1. Show live sensor updates on dashboard.
2. Show serial telemetry/status from ESP32-SENSOR.
3. Trigger a watering action.
4. Show resulting log/history update.

Speaker notes:
- "The goal of the demo is to prove real-time synchronization across edge, backend, and UI."

SLIDE 10 (8:40-10:00) - "Outcomes, Impact, and What Comes Next"
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Key outcomes:
- Real-time multi-sensor monitoring achieved.
- Controlled automated watering implemented.
- AI disease workflow integrated with alerting.
- Modular architecture ready for scaling to more crops/devices.

Closing line:
- "SproutSense demonstrates how low-cost edge hardware and practical AI can improve consistency in plant care."

Q&A BACKUP POINTS
-----------------
- Why dual ESP32 design? Separation of workloads improves stability and maintainability.
- Why no on-device display module in current build? Current workflow prioritizes cloud dashboard and serial diagnostics.
- Why this matters? Faster response, fewer avoidable losses, and a path to smart farming scale.
