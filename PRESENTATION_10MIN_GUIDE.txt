SPROUTSENSE - 10 MIN PRESENTATION (EASY)
========================================

Total time: 10 minutes
Slides: 10
Target: clear and practical demo

SLIDE 1 (0:00-0:40) - Title
---------------------------
SproutSense: AI-powered IoT Plant Care
Say:
- This project automates monitoring, watering, and disease alerting.

SLIDE 2 (0:40-1:20) - Problem
-----------------------------
Say:
- Plants die mostly due to wrong watering and late disease detection.
- Manual care is inconsistent.

SLIDE 3 (1:20-2:20) - Solution Overview
---------------------------------------
Say:
- We built dual ESP32 architecture.
- One ESP32 handles sensors and pump.
- One ESP32-CAM handles disease vision tasks.

SLIDE 4 (2:20-3:20) - Hardware
------------------------------
Show key hardware:
- ESP32 Dev Board
- ESP32-CAM
- DHT22, moisture sensor, pH sensor, LDR, flow sensor
- Relay + pump

SLIDE 5 (3:20-4:20) - System Flow
---------------------------------
Say:
- ESP32 devices send data to backend.
- Backend stores in MongoDB.
- Frontend shows live dashboard.

SLIDE 6 (4:20-5:20) - Smart Watering
------------------------------------
Say:
- Watering starts by threshold logic.
- Safety controls prevent overwatering.
- Logs are saved for each cycle.

SLIDE 7 (5:20-6:30) - TFT Display (ST7735R)
--------------------------------------------
Show TFT live pages:
- Overview page
- Soil + water page
- Climate page
- Alerts + watering history page

Mention wiring:
- CS GPIO5, RST GPIO4, DC GPIO27, MOSI GPIO23, SCLK GPIO18

SLIDE 8 (6:30-7:30) - AI Disease Detection
------------------------------------------
Say:
- ESP32-CAM captures leaf images.
- Disease pipeline identifies risk and pushes alerts.

SLIDE 9 (7:30-8:40) - Live Demo
--------------------------------
Demo flow:
1. Show sensor changes on dashboard
2. Show TFT updates
3. Trigger watering event
4. Show history/log update

SLIDE 10 (8:40-10:00) - Results + Closing
-----------------------------------------
Say:
- Real-time monitoring achieved
- Automated watering implemented
- AI disease workflow integrated
- System is scalable for smart farming

Q&A Backup Points
-----------------
- Why dual ESP32: performance and reliability
- Why TFT: local display without phone/web dependency
- Why this helps: faster action, fewer plant losses
